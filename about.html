About Cyvra

Cyvra AI Security was founded to help small and medium businesses adopt AI safely. Our team blends security engineering, privacy, and applied AI experience to deliver practical programs that fit limited budgets.

Our mission

To make AI security accessible: policy, detection, and managed services for teams who don't have large security staffs.

Our story

We started as consultants for startups and quickly realized SMBs needed a different approach — lightweight governance, automated monitoring, and managed vulnerability operations. Cyvra was created to provide just that.
